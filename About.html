Hotel White Rock: Elegant Oasis in Bangalore



The contemporary and elegant decor of Hotel WHITE ROCK gives tourists a warm and homely welcome. Being close to the market area, WHITE ROCK becomes the first choice for all tourists who are visiting Bangalore for the first time. The hotel offers 24-hour room service.It has an in-house restaurant which serves Indian, Chinese, continental cuisines. Hotel White Rock is the new landmark at the doorway of Bangalore’s growth corridor with well appointed guest rooms, state of the art event space and delicious food and beverage offerings. Fusion of contemporary and modern interiors create a relaxing ambiance, offering guest a spacious and comfortable environment. With our dining options the culinary experience at Hotel White Rock Bangalore is as unique as it is authentic.
